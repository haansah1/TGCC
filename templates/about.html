founder

Daniel Kwame Osae Djan is the founder and General Overseer of the Pleroma International Ministries (PLIM). A product of the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi (KNUST). He has been teaching and ministering to nations and places for the past three years. He lives in the city of London in The United Kingdom. Held the office of the Prayer Secretary as well as Outreach President in The Inter-Hall Christian Fellowship-IHCF whiles on campus. He is an author and writer, counselor, a prophet and a teacher of the word moving strongly in the prophetic giftings.
